Cirntember 2021 Guidelines!

Thank you for your interest in my Cirntember event this year! This page is here to try and set the ground rules for the event and answer any questions you might have.

I will try and keep this page up to date with new questions as they come. If there's anything you need that's not here, please feel free to DM me on Twitter (@CappuCirno) or on Discord (PK#2963).

What is Cirntember?

Cirntember is a month-long event taking place from September 1st - September 30th. Cirno is far too powerful for just one day, so I'm running a month of Cirno content to really celebrate my favourite Touhou character.

The event is hosted primarily through my personal Twitter account, but this year I'm also launching my own Discord server to help co-ordinate things, and just as a nice place to hang out and share good vibes.

=⑨=

What happens in Cirntember?

Every day, a new piece of Cirno-themed content will be posted on my Twitter account at 9PM BST (UTC+1)! Each piece made by a different creator! Last year, this was artwork and photographs, but this year I'm opening submissions to more types of original content.

=⑨=

What kind of content is allowed this year?

This year, submissions will be open for:

ARTWORK (Cirno fan-art made with any medium - digital, analogue, anything!)
ANIMATION (animated work featuring Cirno)
COSPLAY (cosplay work related to Cirno),
PHOTOGRAPHY (photos of Cirno-related things such as merchandise or plushes)
MUSIC (arragement based on one of Cirno's music themes)
PROSE (fiction featuring or related to Cirno)

Each of these categories will come with their own restrictions, please see below.

=⑨=

Can I get involved in Cirntember 2021?

Yes. Absolutely!
If you're interested in getting involved in Cirntember 2021, please fill out the CIRNTEMBER SIGN-UP FORM to register your interest! You're also welcome to DM me if there's anything you need.

=⑨=

Are there any restrictions to submissions?

Yes. There are general restrictions and some for specific media.

General restrictions: All submissions must feature Cirno as a central focus.
• All submissions must be SAFE FOR WORK. This event is for a general audience.
• All submissions must be original content. In the event that a submission uses work from more than one person (such as music used in an animation), permission must be explicitly given by each person involved.
• Existing works re-submitted for the event will be rejected.
